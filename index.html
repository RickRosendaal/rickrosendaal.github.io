Hacksagon is a game about hacking, virusses and firewalls. The player is in his PC, ready to defend himself against hackers.

 

How the game works:

The player needs to get to the other side of the level. He or she will find a firewall there and a hacking system. The hacking system needs to be cracked in order to open te firewall. You'll need numbers for this. These numbers can be found in the level. Remember these digits and fill them in at the hacking system to complete the level.

But that's not all. A hacker is trying to get in your PC and will sent virusses. On the background, you'll see hexagons moving towards the left. To save your PC from virusses, tap the virus to sent it back to the hacker. If the player fails to tap the virus, and the virus reached the left side, the PC health will go down. As soon it hits zero, the player will fade in digits and deleted.

Tap & Turn is a puzzle game where your goal is: Make every tile in the level white. To do so, you must tap tiles, and that tile, as well as the tiles to the left, right, top and bottom will turn along in a "+" shape.

 

The game was created using Game Maker Studio, but later on re-written in C# using Unity.

While doing so, you can use abilities to, for example swap positions with the player in front of you, suddenly making it more likely for you to survive the longest. In the level there are also objects / monsters to be found that slow you or knock you back to make it a bit more difficult to survive.
